The Core Loop

Four systems. One 30-day deadline.

Draft

Every fish you buy or splice is a permanent card in your Ocean. Draft smart, thin the fat, or drown in your own decisions. A bloated deck kills runs.

Survive

Pollution is a run-killer. Hit 3/3 ammonia and the tank crashes - zero income that day. Every draw is tension between greed and survival.

Scale

Eggs buy fish. Shells expand your tank. Crown Fish aren't cheap. Build an engine that scales fast, or time runs out and your fish become corpses.

Incubate

Splice fish to create Finbreds with unpredictable results. Inherit yields, traits, and colors. Embrace or Flush your monstrosities.

How a Run Works

Three phases. Every day. 30 days. No shortcuts.

1
Draw

Draw fish one-by-one from your Ocean into the tank. Maybe it'll be a helpful Pleco. Maybe a Guppy destroys your tank. Push your luck or stop and lock in.

2
Score

End the day to trigger scoring. Every fish in the tank produces Eggs and Shells. Trait bonuses stack. Crashed tanks earn nothing.

3
Hatch

The Hatchery opens. Spend Eggs to draft fish. Spend Shells to expand. Mutate a Finbred in the Incubator. Every choice is permanent.

The Corpse Economy

Some fish eat fish. Some fish profit from carnage. Stack the right roster and watch chain reactions cause havoc.

Flatfish

Predator

Zombie Worm

Bone Eater

Consumes a corpse on entry. +3 Eggs, +2 Shells. Instant payout from a casualty.

Ghostfish

Haunt

+2 Eggs per corpse in the tank at end-of-day. More bones, more income.

A 30-Day Ticking Clock

This is not an idle game. You have exactly 30 days to build an economy that can sustain 3 Crown Fish in the tank on the same day. Buy too early and they dilute your deck. Buy too late and your run might be dead.
